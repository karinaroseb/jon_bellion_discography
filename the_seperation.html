The Seperation

The Separation is Jon Bellion’s 3rd mixtape released December 10th, 2013. There is still very little information about this mixtape or Bellion at the time. However, you can start to see his developing production style. There are still fun upbeat songs like with Translation Through Speakers but more developed lyrics and less corny moments. Bellion’s mixtapes definitely show how far he has grown both lyrically and stylistically.
